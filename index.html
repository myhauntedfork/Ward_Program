Ward Program is plain and simple by design. There are several alternatives for creating a paperless ward bulletin, but they face two main issues:

  • Privacy/Security Issues: Many options include a search feature that allows anyone to locate your ward and view your program. This can expose personal information to individuals who should not have access.
  • Lack of simplicity: Many tools offer excessive customization options, such as fancy fonts and color pickers, which can be overwhelming. Ward Program focuses on simplicity and ease of use, avoiding unnecessary complexities. It's plain as paper!

With Ward Program all you need to do is:

  • Add your content

  • Share you pirvate link or QR code with your ward

  • That's it!
